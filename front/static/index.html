T-Shirt Planning Poker

T-Shirt Planning Poker is a free online tool

Using T-Shirt sizes in our Scrum poker session, allows us to remove analisys parallisis and build consensus estimates.

Get Started in five simple steps:

  1. Choose a name for the ‘master’ player and hit on create session.
  2. A session link will be generated, copy it and share it with the rest of attendees.
  3. Create your story and hit hte start voting button.
  4. Attendees will emit their votes.
  5. Once all attendees has voted, click on finish voting and results
