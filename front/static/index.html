T-Shirt Planning Poker (Beta)

Using T-Shirt sizes in our Scrum poker session, allows us to remove analisys paralysis and reach consensus estimate.

T-Shirt Planning Poker is a free online tool.
How does it work?

Get Started in five simple steps:

  1. Choose a name for the ‘master’ player and hit create session.
  2. A session link will be generated, copy it and share it with the rest of the attendees.
  3. Create your story and hit the start voting button.
  4. Attendees will emit their votes.
  5. Once all attendees have voted, click on finish voting and results will be shown... Create a new story to start the voting process again.
